Technology refers to the application of scientific knowledge for practical purposes, particularly in industries. It encompasses tools, systems, machines, and processes developed to solve problems, improve efficiency, and enhance human life. From simple tools like the wheel to complex innovations like artificial intelligence and space exploration, technology plays a critical role in shaping modern society.
